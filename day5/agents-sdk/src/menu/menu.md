Vegetarian Restaurant Menu

## Entrées

1. Truffle-Infused Mushroom Pâté

A rich and earthy blend of wild mushrooms, black truffle essence, and roasted garlic served with toasted sourdough crisps.

2. Heirloom Beet Carpaccio

Thinly sliced heirloom beets layered with goat cheese mousse, arugula, and candied walnuts, finished with a balsamic reduction.

3. Garden Pea Velouté

Silky-smooth soup of fresh garden peas, mint, and coconut cream, served with a hint of lemon zest and microgreens.

## Main Dishes

1. Wild Mushroom Risotto

Creamy Arborio rice cooked in a white wine reduction, featuring a medley of chanterelles, porcini, and shiitake mushrooms, topped with aged Parmesan shavings.

2. Charred Cauliflower Steak

Thick-cut cauliflower steak grilled to perfection, accompanied by spiced chickpea purée, roasted cherry tomatoes, and tahini drizzle.

3. Spinach and Ricotta Ravioli

Handmade ravioli filled with spinach and ricotta, tossed in a sage-brown butter sauce, sprinkled with toasted pine nuts.

4. Thai Green Curry with Tofu

Fragrant coconut curry featuring tender tofu cubes, baby eggplant, snap peas, and fresh basil, served with jasmine rice.

## Desserts

1. Lavender Crème Brûlée

A delicate custard infused with lavender and vanilla, finished with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust.

2. Dark Chocolate Mousse

Velvety smooth dark chocolate mousse layered with raspberry coulis and topped with gold leaf for a touch of elegance.
